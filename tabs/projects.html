Features:

  • My Account: A personalized hub where users can manage preferences, messages, and security settings. It includes a messaging feature for communication, preferences for customization, and a logout option for account security.
  • Dashboard: Provides real-time data visualization and academic updates, along with a calendar for tracking events.
  • School Setup: A tool for administrators to configure academic parameters like marking periods, calendars, class schedules, courses, and resources for efficient management.
  • Students: A centralized feature for managing student information, including enrollment, academic records, attendance, and disciplinary history. It allows customization of academic settings.
  • Users: A platform for managing user accounts, roles, and permissions. It enhances communication, security, and institutional collaboration while offering reports for decision-making.
  • Scheduling: Manages student and group timetables, course timings, and schedule changes. It includes reporting tools and setup options for scheduling parameters.
  • Grades: Handles student academic records, including report cards, transcripts, and GPA tracking. It ensures grading accuracy and allows customization of grading parameters.
  • Monitoring: Oversees administrative tasks like attendance tracking, report generation, and activity monitoring, ensuring efficiency and accountability in academic operations.
  • Extracurricular: Organizes and manages student extracurricular activities, tracking participation, creating events, and generating reports to enhance student engagement.
  • General Reports: A messaging and communication tool with features like inbox, sent messages, trash, and group messaging, facilitating effective communication within the academic system.
  • Tools: HProvides system management utilities, including access logs, data backups, imports, deletion logs, and reporting tools to maintain system efficiency and security.
